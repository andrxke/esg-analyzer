Our targets are milestoned and specific. We commit to a 50% absolute reduction
by 2030 against the 2019 baseline, an interim checkpoint of 30% by 2027, and
net-zero by 2050. Progress against each interim target is reported annually.

We do not claim carbon neutrality. We report our residual emissions honestly and
describe the specific decarbonization levers, capital allocation, and supplier
engagement programs behind each target so readers can hold us to them.

This report avoids superlatives and states figures with their baselines so that
every claim can be measured against a defined starting point and target year.
